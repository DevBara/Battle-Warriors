Instructions

  1. Press the START button to begin
  2. Players take turns firing shots, there is 1 shot per turn. A shot is accomplished by click on one square.
  3. If you hit the other player ship, you are alloted another turn until you miss.
  4. If you miss the other player ship, your turn ends.
  5. If any player successfully hits the opposite opponent ship, the square will turn red.
  6. The first player to sink all 3 of their opponent ships wins the game.
